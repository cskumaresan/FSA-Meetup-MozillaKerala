about the meetup

The event will be a gathering of FSA's who are interested to contribute as well as interested to Share things about Mozilla . Mostly FSA's in and around of Kerala will be joining the event. It is expected to have 100+ FSA's at the event. The event mainly aims to educate FSA's clearly with the much needed , " What is Mozilla , it's Mission , and it's various products , projects and initiatives " . The event will be a perfect kick start for FSA's to spread the word and to contribute. FSAs will learn about next step towards contribution to Mozilla :)
